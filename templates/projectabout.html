Surplus Food Management

Guidelines

This website is about donating the excess food to the Orphanages

Steps to be followed by Donor

Donor have to fill the donor form

submit the details,and collect the certificate

The orphanages will contact the donor

Steps to be followed by Orphanage

The Orphanage first register in the site

logins will be actived ,after approval of admin(to verify the organization)

orphanage can login into donation tab,where all donation details are available

The required orphanage can contact to particular donor,and before contacting they have to click on request button
